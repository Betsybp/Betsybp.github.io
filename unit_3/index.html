Women Helping Other Women Succeed

We are an all-volunteer service club for professional women. We donate time, financial resources, and energy in ways which enable other women and girls to experience the joy of pursuing their personal and professional dreams.

You are a good fit for our club if you:

  • Want to network with other professional women in the San Fernando Valley
  • Are passionate about expanding opportunities for women and girls locally and globally
  • See yourself growing into a leadership position
