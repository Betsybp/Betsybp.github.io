Soroptimist International of San Fernando Valley is a volunteer service club for professional women. We donate time, financial resources, and energy to improve the lives of women and girls through programs leading to social and economic empowerment.

Join the Club

There is power in numbers, and together we accomplish more. If you are a strong, capable woman who wants to leave her mark on the world then we want you! We offer flexible involvement opportunities - donate a couple of hours a month, or dive right in and grow into a leadership position. The choice is yours.

Make a Donation

Your donation to our club will directly support the programs you find most meaningful. Whichever program you choose, your gift will make a big difference for the women and girls in need in our community.
